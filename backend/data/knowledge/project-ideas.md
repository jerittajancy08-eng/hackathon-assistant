# Hackathon Project Ideas

Category: Project Ideas
Source: internal-hackbot-guide

## AI

- AI study mentor that turns course notes into quizzes, summaries, and personalized revision plans.
- Resume and interview coach that scores answers and suggests role-specific improvements.
- Smart meeting assistant that extracts actions, risks, owners, and deadlines.
- Local language health assistant that explains basic symptoms and recommends professional care when needed.

## Web3

- Donation transparency dashboard using wallet transactions and milestone proofs.
- DAO proposal summarizer that explains governance proposals in plain language.
- Credential wallet for hackathon certificates, badges, and verifiable achievements.
- Micro-grant platform for open-source contributors.

## Healthcare

- Appointment triage assistant for clinics that routes patients by urgency and department.
- Medicine reminder and caregiver alert app.
- AI-powered report explainer that turns medical reports into patient-friendly summaries.
- Rural telehealth intake system with multilingual support.

## FinTech

- Expense anomaly detector for students and freelancers.
- Financial literacy coach that explains savings, taxes, and credit basics.
- Fraud pattern dashboard for small merchants.
- Subscription tracker that identifies unused recurring payments.

## Smart Cities

- Citizen issue reporting app with location, photo evidence, and prioritization.
- Public transport crowding predictor.
- Waste pickup optimization dashboard.
- Flood or heat risk alert system using public data.

## Education

- Personalized learning path generator.
- Peer study group matcher.
- Assignment feedback assistant.
- Teacher dashboard that identifies struggling students from quiz patterns.

Strong hackathon ideas usually have a clear user, a painful problem, a small demoable MVP, measurable impact, and a story judges can understand in under one minute.
