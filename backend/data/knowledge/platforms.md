# Hackathon Platforms

## Devpost
Category: Hackathon Platforms
Source: https://devpost.com/hackathons

Devpost is a major hackathon discovery and hosting platform for online, in-person, student, corporate, and themed hackathons. It is useful for finding beginner-friendly events, prize tracks, rules, judging criteria, submission requirements, and project galleries.

Use Devpost when a participant wants broad hackathon discovery, project inspiration, submission examples, or a place to host a project portfolio.

Official link: https://devpost.com/hackathons

## Major League Hacking (MLH)
Category: Hackathon Platforms
Source: https://mlh.io

MLH supports student and community hackathons, especially university-focused events. It is useful for beginners because MLH events often provide workshops, mentors, starter resources, community support, and structured event calendars.

Use MLH when a student wants a beginner-friendly hackathon path, campus events, or community-based learning.

Official link: https://mlh.io

## ETHGlobal
Category: Hackathon Platforms
Source: https://ethglobal.com

ETHGlobal runs Ethereum and Web3-focused hackathons, summits, and builder events. It is best for blockchain, smart contract, wallet, DeFi, DAO, NFT, ZK, and decentralized application projects.

Use ETHGlobal when a team wants Web3 mentors, sponsor prizes, protocol-specific tracks, and serious blockchain builders.

Official link: https://ethglobal.com

## Hack2Skill
Category: Hackathon Platforms
Source: https://hack2skill.com

Hack2Skill hosts hackathons, innovation challenges, corporate challenges, and developer competitions. It is useful for students and professionals looking for structured problem statements, industry themes, and challenge-based events.

Use Hack2Skill when a participant wants theme-based challenges, corporate innovation events, or India-friendly hackathon discovery.

Official link: https://hack2skill.com

## DoraHacks
Category: Hackathon Platforms
Source: https://dorahacks.io

DoraHacks is a global hacker movement and platform for hackathons, bounties, grants, and Web3 ecosystem events. It is strong for blockchain communities, open-source ecosystems, and project funding opportunities.

Use DoraHacks when a team wants Web3 bounties, ecosystem grants, blockchain hackathons, or ongoing funding opportunities.

Official link: https://dorahacks.io

## Unstop
Category: Hackathon Platforms
Source: https://unstop.com

Unstop hosts hackathons, case competitions, hiring challenges, quizzes, and student competitions. It is popular for college events, corporate competitions, and career-linked challenges.

Use Unstop when a student wants hackathons connected to colleges, companies, career opportunities, and structured competition rounds.

Official link: https://unstop.com
